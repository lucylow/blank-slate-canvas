# Circuit of the Americas (COTA) - Comprehensive Data Analysis Report

**Date:** November 20, 2025  
**Track:** Circuit of the Americas (COTA)  
**Data Source:** Official GR Cup Race Data (Races 1 & 2)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the Circuit of the Americas (COTA) race weekend, expanding on the initial findings from Race 1. The analysis integrates data from both Race 1 and Race 2, covering race results, weather conditions, sector performance, and lap times. 

The COTA race weekend was characterized by intense competition, with a large field of 31 drivers. Race 1 saw a dominant performance from car #46, while Race 2 featured a different winner and even tighter competition at the front. The data reveals that COTA, with its mix of high-speed straights and technical corners, rewards both outright pace and strategic consistency.

## 4. Comparative Analysis & Key Takeaways

1.  **High Level of Competition:** Both races featured extremely close finishes, with the top drivers separated by fractions of a second. This highlights the spec nature of the series and the high level of driver talent.

2.  **Consistency is King:** While car #46 had the fastest lap in Race 1 and won, car #21 had the fastest lap in Race 2 but finished P4. The winners of both races demonstrated exceptional consistency in their lap times, even if they didn't always have the single fastest lap.

3.  **Winning at COTA:** To succeed at COTA, a driver needs a combination of:
    *   **Qualifying Pace:** Starting at the front is a significant advantage in such a competitive field.
    *   **Technical Skill:** The ability to navigate the complex sequences of corners in Sector 2 is where the most time can be gained or lost.
    *   **Racecraft:** With such close racing, the ability to manage traffic and make decisive overtaking maneuvers is crucial.

4.  **Strategic Implications:** The data from both races suggests that a two-pronged strategy is required:
    *   **Qualifying:** Maximize single-lap pace to secure a top grid position.
    *   **Race:** Focus on hitting consistent lap times and minimizing mistakes. The race is more likely to be won through consistency than through a single heroic lap.

## 5. Conclusion & Recommendations

This expanded analysis of the COTA race weekend reinforces the initial findings and provides a more complete picture of the racing dynamics at this challenging circuit. The key recommendations for teams are:

- **Data-Driven Setup:** Use the sector analysis data to optimize car setup for the technical middle sector. A car that is agile and responsive in the esses will have a significant advantage.
- **Driver Focus:** Coach drivers on the importance of consistency. Use the lap time data to identify and analyze any laps where significant time was lost.
- **Strategy Planning:** Emphasize the importance of qualifying. In a race with such tight margins, track position is paramount. Plan for a race strategy that prioritizes clean air and consistent lap times.

By focusing on these key areas, teams can leverage the insights from this data analysis to improve their performance and achieve better results at the Circuit of the Americas.
